CONSENT TO PARTICIPATE IN A RESEARCH STUDY

RESEARCH STUDY: I have been asked to participate in a research study under the direction of Dr. Quentin Jones. Other professional persons who work with them as study staff may assist to act for them.

PURPOSE: To evaluate the usability of an online dating system prototype.

DURATION: My participation in this study will last for approximately 3 hours.

PROCEDURES: I have been told that, during the course of this study, the following will occur: I will exchange messages with opposite-sex users of the online dating system prototype and then meet them face-to-face for speed dates.

EXCLUSIONS: I will inform the researcher if any of the following apply to me: I am already in a committed romantic relationship with another person. I am under the age of 18.
